GLB privacy notice

This Privacy Notice applies to Enterprise Data Integration and is being provided on behalf of its affiliates listed below ("we"), as required by the Gramm-Leach Bliley Act. This Notice describes how we collect, share, and protect nonpublic personal information (NPI).

Read the privacy notice

Collecting information

We collect NPI about our customers to provide them with investment services. The types of NPI we collect for these purposes may include user name and email.

Sharing information

We don't share NPI.

Safeguarding information

We have physical, electronic and procedural safeguards that protect the confidentiality and security of NPI. We give access only to employees who need to know the NPI to provide services to you.

Access to information

You may request access to certain NPI we collect to provide you with services. You must make your request in writing, providing your full name and address to the address below. We will reply within 30 business days of receipt. If you request, we will send copies of the NPI to you or make available to you at our office. We will also send you information related to disclosures. We may charge a reasonable fee to cover our copying costs.

Correction of information

If you believe the NPI we have about you is incorrect, please write to us and include your full name, address, telephone number, and the reason you believe the NPI is inaccurate. We will reply within 30 business days of receipt. If we agree with you, we will correct the NPI and notify you.

If we disagree with you, we will tell you we are not going to make the correction and the reason(s) for our refusal. We will also tell you that you may submit a statement to us. Your statement should include the NPI you believe is correct and the reason(s) why you disagree with our decision not to correct the NPI in our files. We will file your statement with the disputed NPI to be accessible. We will include your statement any time the disputed NPI is reviewed or disclosed.

Contacting us

For additional information about our privacy, please visit: edi.works/privacy_notice.

We reserve the right to modify this notice. We will provide you with a new notice if we make material changes to our privacy practices.
